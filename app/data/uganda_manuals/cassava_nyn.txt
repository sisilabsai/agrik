RUNYANKOLE VERSION (STARTER TRANSLATION)
EKYOKURIMA: CASSAVA

OBUTUURE
- Shabaashaba omwanya ogurungi.
- Koresha ebishere ezirungi.

ENZURURU N'OBURWARA (OBUMANYISO)
- Cassava mosaic: amababi gakyendera, okukura kutari.
- Brown streak: okuhinduka kwa langi n’obufo mu mirandira.

AMAGARA AGABONYEZA
- Ggyamu ebimera ebirwadde.
- Koresha ebika ebirina obuggya ku burwara.
