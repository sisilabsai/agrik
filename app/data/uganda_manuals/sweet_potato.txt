UGANDA EXTENSION MANUAL (EXPANDED)
CROP: SWEET POTATO (Ipomoea batatas)

LOCAL VARIETAL NOTES (VERIFY LOCALLY)
- Orange-fleshed varieties are preferred for nutrition where available.

REGIONAL DIFFERENCES (UGANDA)
- Eastern/Northern: drought-tolerant varieties are favored.
- Central: varieties for fresh markets and processing.

PLANTING
- Plant vines at onset of rains.
- Spacing: 75-90 cm between rows, 25-30 cm within rows.

PEST AND DISEASE SYMPTOM GUIDE
- Sweet potato weevil: holes and tunnels in roots.
- Virus diseases: leaf distortion and stunting.

MANAGEMENT (IPM)
- Use clean vines and rotate fields.
- Harvest on time to reduce weevil damage.

HARVEST
- Harvest 3-5 months after planting, depending on variety.
