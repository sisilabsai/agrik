EKYOKURIMA: MAIZE

OBUTUURE
- Shabaashaba omwanya ogurungi, ogutahika amaizi.
- Koresha embuto enungi.
- Shanga ku ntandikwa y’enkuba.

ENZURURU N'OBURWARA (OBUMANYISO)
- Fall armyworm: ebishushani mu mababi, akasasa mu mutwe.
- Maize streak: layini za kyenvu mu mababi.

AMAGARA AGABONYEZA
- Reba ennimiro buli wiiki.
- Kora IPM kandi oshubye ebirime.
