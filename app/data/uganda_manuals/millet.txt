UGANDA EXTENSION MANUAL (EXPANDED)
CROP: MILLET (Pearl millet / Finger millet)

LOCAL VARIETAL NOTES (VERIFY LOCALLY)
- Choose varieties suitable to your zone and market use.

REGIONAL DIFFERENCES (UGANDA)
- Northern/Eastern: drought-tolerant types perform well.
- Highlands: finger millet with good disease tolerance.

PLANTING
- Plant at onset of rains.
- Spacing: 45-60 cm between rows, thin plants to 10-15 cm.

PEST AND DISEASE SYMPTOM GUIDE
- Blast: gray/brown lesions on leaves and heads.
- Birds: grain loss near harvest.

MANAGEMENT (IPM)
- Use clean seed and timely weeding.
- Harvest promptly to reduce bird damage.

HARVEST
- Harvest when heads are dry and grain is hard.
