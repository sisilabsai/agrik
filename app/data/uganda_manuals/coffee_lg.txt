LUGANDA VERSION (STARTER TRANSLATION)
EKIKULI: KOFI (COFFEE)

ENFA Y'OKULIMA
- Londa ttaka eririna obusoso bungi, n’akafumu.
- Simbira ku ntandikwa y’enkuba.

EBIMULI N'OBULWADDE (EBIMANYISO)
- Coffee wilt: okukkala n’okufa okw’amangu.
- Coffee berry disease: obubonero obwa kizibu ku bibala.

AMAGEZI
- Sala emiti buli kiseera.
- Kozesa ebika ebirina obuggya ku bulwadde.
