UGANDA EXTENSION MANUAL (EXPANDED)
CROP: SORGHUM (Sorghum bicolor)

LOCAL VARIETAL NOTES (VERIFY LOCALLY)
- Choose drought-tolerant varieties for drier zones.
- Confirm current recommended varieties with district extension offices.

REGIONAL DIFFERENCES (UGANDA)
- Northern/Eastern: prioritize early-maturing, drought-tolerant types.
- Western highlands: manage for cooler conditions and bird pressure.

PLANTING
- Plant at onset of rains.
- Spacing: 60-75 cm between rows, 15-20 cm between plants.

PEST AND DISEASE SYMPTOM GUIDE
- Striga: stunted plants, poor grain fill.
- Bird damage: missing grain heads near harvest.
- Smut: black powdery heads.

MANAGEMENT (IPM)
- Use clean seed and rotate crops.
- Timely weeding to reduce Striga.

HARVEST
- Harvest when grain is hard and panicles are dry.
