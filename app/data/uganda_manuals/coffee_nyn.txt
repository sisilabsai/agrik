RUNYANKOLE VERSION (STARTER TRANSLATION)
EKYOKURIMA: COFFEE

OBUTUURE
- Shabaashaba omwanya ogurungi n’obusoso.
- Shanga ku ntandikwa y’enkuba.

ENZURURU N'OBURWARA (OBUMANYISO)
- Coffee wilt: okufa okw’amangu.
- Coffee berry disease: ebishushani ku bibala.

AMAGARA AGABONYEZA
- Twese emiti buli kiseera.
- Koresha ebika ebirina obuggya ku burwara.
