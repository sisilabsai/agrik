UGANDA EXTENSION MANUAL (EXPANDED)
CROP: RICE (Oryza sativa)

LOCAL VARIETAL NOTES (VERIFY LOCALLY)
- Lowland and upland varieties differ; choose per field type.

REGIONAL DIFFERENCES (UGANDA)
- Eastern lowlands: lowland rice is common.
- Western/Northern uplands: upland varieties may be preferred.

PLANTING
- Plant at onset of rains or transplant for lowland systems.
- Spacing: 20-25 cm between rows (transplanted).

PEST AND DISEASE SYMPTOM GUIDE
- Rice blast: lesions on leaves and panicles.
- Stem borers: dead hearts in vegetative stage.

MANAGEMENT (IPM)
- Use clean seed and field hygiene.
- Maintain proper water management in lowlands.

HARVEST
- Harvest when grains are hard and panicles are yellow.
